Tip #1: Paint-By-Number Puzzles   
The image above is a paint-by-number puzzle. Your goal is to discover a hidden picture by filling in the grid cells in a way that agrees with the clue numbers along the side and top. You fill in cells by clicking on them. One click makes a dot appear, marking the cell as definitely being white. Click again and another color appears.
